Elephants are the giants of the Jungle. They are mammals and the largest land animals that still exist.

There are three species: The African Bush Elephant, The African Forest Elephant, and the Asian Elephant.

Two elephant species that are extinct are mammoths and straight-tusked elephants. Elephants have long trunks, tusks, large ears, long legs, and tough skin.
