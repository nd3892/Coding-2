Sustainability in Daily Life

Earth's climate is in a dire state, due to human activity. Sometimes, it can be hard not to feel helpless when you can't directly contribute to a solution to climate change.

This website is dedicated to sharing tips and tricks for living a more sustainable lifestyle!

One of the main things you can do to reduce your carbon foorprint is reducing physical waste production. The Zero Waste movement is one that encourages the individual to reduce their waste in order to conserve energy and our planet.
